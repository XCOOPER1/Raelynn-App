Alan AI Voice Commands

This section explains the various voice commands you can use with the Raelynn Ordering App and what they do:

  • Order Drinks:
    • Say "Can I have a drink?" to hear the available drinks. The options will be highlighted as they are read out.
    • Say "Get me a [drink name]", "One [drink name], please", or "I need/want a [drink name]" to add a drink to your order.
  • Order Snacks:
    • Say "Get me a [snack name]", "One [snack name], please", or "I need/want a [snack name]" to add a snack to your order.
  • Provide Your Name:
    • Say "My name is [your name]" to input your name into the order form.
  • Provide Your Address:
    • Say "My address is [your address]" to input your address into the order form.
  • Greetings and Information:
    • Say "Hello Ray Ray" or "Hello" to receive a greeting.
    • Say "What is your name?" to hear the bot's name.
    • Say "Mommy" or "mom" to hear a description of the user's mom.
  • Interactive Games:
    • Say "Do you want to play with me?" or "Play with me" to start a game.
    • Say "Do you like music?" or "Sing with me" to hear the bot sing a song.
    • Say "Do you want to play hide and seek?" or "Hide and seek" to start a hide and seek game. The bot will guide you through the game.
    • Say "Tell me a joke" or "Make me laugh" to hear a joke.
    • Say "Do you want to play a guessing game?" or "Guessing game" to play an animal guessing game.
    • Say "Do you want to play Simon Says?" or "Simon Says" to play Simon Says.
    • Say "Can you sing a song?" or "Sing a song" to hear the bot sing "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star."
    • Say "Tell me a fun fact" or "Fun fact" to hear an interesting fact.
    • Say "Do you want to play trivia?" or "Trivia game" to play a trivia game.
  • Favorite Song and Pink Dog:
    • Say "Play my favorite song" or "Play my song" to play a favorite song. If it does not work, you might need to play it manually.
    • Say "Show the pink dog" or "Show me the pink dog" to display the pink dog image.
    • Say "Hide the pink dog" or "Hide the pink dog" to hide the pink dog image.
  • Miscellaneous:
    • Say "Where is the dog" to hear a response about the dog's location, with a humorous twist.

Enjoy interacting with the Raelynn Ordering App using these voice commands!
